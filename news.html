Our demonstration would involve vehicles that have our RapidEMS program loaded and radios to simulate communication between vehicles and an ambulance. There are several models for traffic simulation. Research has shown that microscopic models that model the behavior of individual vehicles yield realistic behavior. Microscopic models are models that focus on individual assessment of an object, vehicle assessment in our case. However, a macroscopic model that focuses on all our vehicles would be resourceful to simulate the real world and its effects on our system.
