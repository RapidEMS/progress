ABOUT

Our proposal focuses on attempting to rectify the inefficiencies in current ambulance route planning and public awareness in modern EMS systems. By developing a systematic protocol, our initiative not only aims to optimize ambulance routes for quicker response times but also emphasizes the importance of public awareness through strategic communication and public knowledge, we aim to empower the community with the knowledge necessary to facilitate a more cooperative and informed role in emergency response.
